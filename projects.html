Projects

Analysis of Human Errors in Warehouses

Conducted during my MSc in Logistics and Supply Chain Management, this project focused on analyzing human errors in warehouse operations to identify bottlenecks and improve efficiency. Data analysis was performed using IBM SPSS and Microsoft Excel, leveraging pivot tables for detailed insights and trend identification.

Convertible Wheelchair Design Project

Designed a wheelchair attachable to an electric-powered hand bike, allowing enhanced mobility and functionality for users. The project involved creating detailed 3D models using SOLIDWORKS, considering ergonomics, stability, and ease of assembly.

Optimization Parameters to Minimize Squeal Generation

B-Tech final year project studying squeal generation in disc brakes under unidirectional sliding between brake pads and discs. Composites were cast using stir casting, specimens were manufactured using WEDM, and testing was performed on a pin-on-disc tribometer to optimize design parameters and reduce noise.

Fabrication of Water Cooler cum Heater

During my diploma, I fabricated a compact water cooler cum heater unit, demonstrating practical skills in assembly, material selection, and thermal management.
